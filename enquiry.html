How You Can Get Involved

NLF consistently hires graduates to work as New Leaders Consultants (Project Managers) across seven provinces. We create opportunities for professionals to engage in purposeful work that drives change in South Africa. If you are passionate about education and data, we want to hear from you.

NGO & Government Partnerships

NLF partners with NGOs, government departments, and international organisations. If your work touches education, data, or community development, explore how a partnership with NLF could amplify your impact and ours.
