About New Leaders Foundation

New Leaders Foundation (NLF) is a non-profit organisation committed to transforming South African education. Founded in 2009, we have been working to build a culture of data-driven decision-making through our Data Driven Districts (DDD) programme.

Our History

New Leaders Foundation was founded in 2009 by Giles Gillett and is registered as a non-profit company (NPC) in South Africa. The organisation was built on a core conviction: that education and unemployment are two of the greatest challenges facing our generation, and that data placed in the right hands can drive meaningful, measurable change.

In 2012, NLF partnered with the Michael & Susan Dell Foundation and the Department of Basic Education to develop the Data Driven Districts (DDD) programme. The programme collects school-level data through the South African School Administration and Management System (SA-SAMS) and visualises it on the DDD Dashboard, providing education officials with both micro-level (individual learner) and macro-level (district-wide) insights.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To transform South African education by developing empowered leaders and embedding a culture of data-driven decision-making within the public education system, so that every learner's potential can be realised.

Our Vision

A thriving education ecosystem where every learner's potential is fully realised and every young South African has access to quality education regardless of geography or socio-economic background.
